Code School

The Code School Challenge offers year 7-8s the opportunity to show off their team working, design and presentation skills in a development setting at Plymouth University. The outcome of the day is to create and present a Micro Bit application or game. Teams of 5 students are invited to attend Plymouth University to pit their wits against each other. In order to create the best and most unique application.

Micro Bit Lessons

The theme of this years Code School is the BBC Micro bit. If you happen to get stuck or can't find and ideas you would like to develop feel free, to browse this selection of applications. Make sure you pick your editor before hand!
